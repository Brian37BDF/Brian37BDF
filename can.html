About the Project

As remote learning boomed in 2020, I worked with the team at Elevated Third to rethink the way that Instructure would market their LMS system worldwide with a freshly designed lead-gen platform. The Canvas products are used by educators and students of all levels, so it was important to differentiate them via “audience” and “need” pages with distinct themes to help tell them apart.  Being an international site, it was definitely an interesting challenge designing for multiple languages in the same layouts- solved with some clever dynamic font resizing tricks and content writing guidance provided to the client team.
