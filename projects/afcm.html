Landing Pages:

While working at American Financing, one of my main responsibilities was often creating new landing pages for various marketing efforts across the web, tv, and print. When I first started, I helped the company move existing pages from Unbounce to Optimizely, two different A/B testing platforms. From then on I was able to use Optimizely to further enhance our pages based on my experiments with certain variables like photo selection, copy changes, or CTA color swaps. I also developed an entire style guide/building kit for future landing page production so that the other designers on my team could also create new pages if they needed, which you can read about on the AFC tools page here.

Some landing pages built with my style-guide.
