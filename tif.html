About the Project

TiFi is looking to build a digital mailbox to manage your bills.  This means providing the user with clear knowledge of their spend and full control over what bills to pay, when.  We’ve been able to provide this in our new app by allowing users to scan, email, or manually enter their bill information to sort them all in one place.  We’ve provided a safe platform for making payments using typical methods like ACH transfer or Apple Pay, or the app can just be used for record keeping and to forecast future spend via our analytics tools.

Just launched on the App Store and coming soon to Google Play.

Currently working on a bill sharing feature to manage bills with other users!

The home screen of the app where you can view upcoming bills and review recent ones.
Quickly pay or file your bills using info pulled via optical character recognition.
Link your bank account, ApplePay, or use TiFi wallet credits to pay your recurring bills.
A suite of analytics tools provide a birds-eye view of your expenses by the month and year.
Drill down into categories or custom tags for a more nuanced view of bills within.
Newly added swipe functionality will allow quicker bill intake on the review screen.
