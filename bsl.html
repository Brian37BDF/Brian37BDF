About the Project

After a major brand redesign, Benchmark came to Elevated Third for help reworking the web experience of all of the locations under their banner.  Based in the Northeast, Benchmark provides top-class independent living, assisted living, mind and memory and respite care options. Given the multiple sub-brands that would share this over-arching web structure, we needed to ensure the reusability of components between each site while also providing the customizability to give each their own look and feel.
