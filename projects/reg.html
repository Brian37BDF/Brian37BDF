Created a modern and exciting book shopping experience for a NYC-based publisher.
About this project:

While working at Ronik Design, we worked with a game-changing book publisher named Regan Arts to build them a fresh e-commerce shop and blog that could match up visually to their bold book cover designs. My focus was primarily on wireframing and mocking up their catalog page, as well designing a captivating way to showcase excerpts, images, and reviews on each dedicated product page.
