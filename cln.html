About the Project

After undergoing a significant rebrand, Clean Earth was ready for a new website that could produce leads and continue to grow the business. Clean Earth is one of the largest specialty waste companies in the US, operating a network of hazardous waste treatment, storage and disposal facilities. The goal I had in mind while redesigning the website was to break up the walls of text on their previous site into more digestible categories and sections. Matching this with a lightweight color scheme and their rich brand photography made it a success!
