Developed the look & feel for a new kiteboarding school in Puerto Rico.
About this project:

Met two folks looking to open up a kiteboarding school in Puerto Rico and needing a brand design. Helped lead the conversation by researching and creating mood boards, then collaborated with the client to find a style that represented them. Next I developed several iterations of logo design, type treatment, and color schemes; and finally advised the team on how to configure their Shopify page to match this new system.

Mood Board:

The first order of business was to develop some potential styles I could use to guide the clients in discussion of their future look & feel. I did this by breaking down 3 different collections of screenshots, color swatches, photos, fonts, icons, etc. that I found in my research into three categories: action, tropical, and sleek. The first direction plays on the adrenaline rush of kitesurfing by utilizing lots of extreme photography and video to project the thrill of the experience. Bold san-serif fonts with bright accent colors can create the contrast needed to cut through these more media-rich layouts. The second direction, Tropical, puts all of the focus on the beauty of the scenery. The colors reflect the nature of the Caribbean, featuring photos and patterns of waves, sunsets and palm leaves. The final direction, Sleek, takes a more elegant twist, featuring thin san-serif fonts paired with a minimal layout and presentation.
