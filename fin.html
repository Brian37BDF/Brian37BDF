About the Project

As one of the last projects I worked on at Elevated Third, I'm glad that I had such exciting new brand elements to work with while transforming the digital experience of this global fintech leader. By simplifying the content architecture and ensuring all pages had proper conversion points, we knew that visitors were able to find what they needed regardless of where they were landing on the site. By leaning into the dramatic new palette provided by the client, we were able to include eye catching headers and high contrast moment to call attention to key elements to make the experience stand out from their competitors.
